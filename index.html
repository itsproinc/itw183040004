About Us

We are an indie game developer!

Know more about us

Bersow Games an indie game development company founded and ran by two people. It was originally founded in 2013 under a different name by Bakhtiar, the lead coder and Siong Yhi, the lead artist and lead animator.

company name is derived from a combination of the proto-Germanic rune of ‘Berkana’ and ‘Sowilō’, we chose to derive our name from ‘Berkana’ and ‘Sowilō’ because it represented a good start and success in our work respectively. We came to the conclusion of choosing this name after weeks of thinking of a meaningful name.

Outpost Echo

Outpost Echo is our take on a tower defense game with our very own twists. Work towards a safer land as you build traps, build walls, gather resources and expand to compensate for more and more buildings needed to keep up the defense against the enemies.

Neon Cubes

Progress

Neon Cubes

Neon Cubes is a top-down-shooter where you as a cube try to escape the facility and evacute the area immediately, but there will be fighting on your way so equip and be prepare to fight.

Hacker Tapper

Progress

Hacker Tapper

Hacker tapper is a classic tap tycoon game where you earn money by tapping the screen and not to forget the further you are the harder the enemies, thats where you need to save up and upgrade your weapon and armor to advance the game.

Rogue Intelligence

Progress

Rogue Intelligence

Rogue Intelligence is a tap tycoon game where you tap the crystal to earn money, with the money collected you can upgrade your crystal so that it will drop more cash per click.

The Final Artifact

Progress

The Final Artifact

The Final Artifact is a rogue like dungeon crawler game where the dungeon are randomly generated and you have to survive in the dungeon as long as you can.
